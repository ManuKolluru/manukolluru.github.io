Carbon Emission Trends in the United States

Manu Kolluru, Trip Bastian, John Kathe

The goal of our data visualization project was to find the development of and changes in carbon emissions in the United States since 1990 as well present these developments for viewers to examine and interact with in a quick and intuitive way. Considering the public issue climate change in the past few decades, this set of data visualization will allow viewers to see how the United States is pogressing or digressing in the pursuit of decreasing carbon emissions.
